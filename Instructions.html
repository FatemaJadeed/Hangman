Hangman Game

Welcome to the Hangman game! The objective is to guess the hidden word before the hangman is fully drawn

How to Play:

  • You will be presented with a hidden word in the form of underscores, where each underscore represents a letter.
  • Guess the letters that might be in the word.
  • If the guessed letter is correct, it will be revealed in its correct position.
  • If the guessed letter is incorrect, you lose a try and part of the hangman will be drawn.
  • You have a limited number of tries (typically 6) to guess the word correctly before the game is over.
  • If you manage to guess the word before running out of tries, you win!
  • Surprise Events:
    • At random moments, you may encounter surprise events like:

    • Recovering a lost try
    • Skipping a wrong letter without losing a try
  • Timed Guesses:
    • You have a limited amount of time for each guess.
    • If you don't select a letter in time, you lose a try!
